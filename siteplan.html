Rapid Adventures Rafting Site Plan

Prepared by Otu Maduabuchi Okah

Site Name

Rapid Adventures Rafting

This name represents excitement, energy, and the thrill of whitewater rafting while emphasizing adventure and safety.

Optional domain: rapidadventuresrafting.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote outdoor rafting experiences and provide information about trip packages, safety guidelines, team details, and online booking for family and group adventures.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a group rafting trip for my friends next weekend?
  • What should I bring on a first-time rafting trip?
  • Where can I find photos and reviews from previous guests?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0077b6 — used for headers, navigation, and titles.

Accent Color: #90e0ef — used for highlights, buttons, and borders.

These colors represent water, freshness, and adventure — matching the rafting theme perfectly.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (for headers and titles)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (for paragraphs and general text)

The fonts are easy to read and modern, reflecting a professional outdoor adventure company style.
